Eliminating Aliases
If a message indicates that one or more genes or their aliases have been found, follow
these steps:
1.
Select the appropriate option from the drop-down list.
To end the search, click Cancel button.
2.
To continue, click the Go button.
For information about the Gene Expression Plot, see Understanding a Gene Expression Plot.
