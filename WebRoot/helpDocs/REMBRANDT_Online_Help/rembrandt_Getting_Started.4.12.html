Logging Out
To log out of REMBRANDT, follow these steps.
1.
On the REMBRANDT workspace, click the logout link in the upper right-hand corner.
The Logout page appears.
Select one of the following options:
•
To return to REMBRANDT, select Continue working in the application and do not logout.
•
To log out of REMBRANDT without saving the session, select Do not save my current session and logout.
•
To log out and save your session, select Save my current session and logout.
2.
To fill out a three-question survey, click Click Here to take a quick feedback survey. Answer the questions.
3.
Click the Submit button.
