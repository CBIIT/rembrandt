Class Comparison Report
The Class Comparison report displays group average, fold change, and p-value based on the Advanced Search parameters that you selected. The output varies based on the statistical method chosen.
For a T-test or Wilcoxon Statistical Method analysis, the Class Comparison report is as follows.
•
The report displays the group average, where the numerator is the mean of log(base 2) expression signals from the samples in the first group. The denominator is the mean of log(base 2) expression signals from the samples in the second group.
•
The fold change for the reporter between the selected groups appears along with p-value.
•
Gene symbol annotations appear for each reporter. To obtain extensive annotations, click the Excel icon on the upper right-hand corner of the report.
For an F-test Statistical Method analysis, the difference is that in the results there is a group average displayed for each group.
