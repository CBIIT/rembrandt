Logging In
To log into REMBRANDT, you need your username and password assigned to you by the REMBRANDT Administrator.
Note:
To create a REMBRANDT user account, see Creating a User Account.
1.
On the login page, enter your User Name and Password.
If you would like to offer feedback via e-mail to the REMBRANDT devel­opment team, click the feedback link.
2.
Click the Submit button. If your login is successful, the Legal Rules of the Road page appears.
