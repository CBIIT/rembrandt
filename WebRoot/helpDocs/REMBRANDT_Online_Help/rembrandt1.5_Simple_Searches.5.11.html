Redrawing the K-M Survival Plot for Gene Expression Data
To redraw a K-M Gene Expression data, follow these steps:
Note:
If you restricted the search to a group, Constrained to group appears at the top.
1.
To dynamically modify the fold change thresholds and redraw the plot, adjust the Up-Regulated and Down-Regulated values.
2.
To visualize the K-M plot for the unified probeset, select a value from the Reporters drop-down list.
3.
Specify a Unified or a Affymetrix Reporter Selection.
4.
Click the Redraw Graph button.
For more information, see Understanding K-M Survival Plot for Gene Expression Data.
