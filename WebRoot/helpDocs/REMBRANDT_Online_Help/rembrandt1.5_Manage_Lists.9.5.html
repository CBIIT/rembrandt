Deleting Entire Lists
You may delete an entire list. Follow these steps:
1.
Select the list type from the list menu beneath the function tabs at the top of the page. All the lists in that category display.
1.
Select a list by clicking the box beside it.
2.
Click the x beside delete.
3.
Your selected list will be removed. (Need a figure to show this?)
