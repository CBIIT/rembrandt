Deleting Items From a List
You may delete items from an existing list, then view the new list or save the existing list on your computer. Follow these steps:
1.
Select the category list type from the list menu beneath the function tabs at the top of the page. Lists for that category appear.
2.
Select a list by clicking the box beside the list.
3.
Click the details icon to view all items in the list.
4.
Click the box beside the item(s) you want to delete.That item will be deleted from the list. (Figure: Click the Details Icon to Delete a List Item)
5.
Click the export link at the bottom of the items list to open and view the new list or save the list on your computer.
