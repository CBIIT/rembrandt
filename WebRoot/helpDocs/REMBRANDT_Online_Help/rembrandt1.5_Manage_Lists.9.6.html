Adding a New List
You may add a new list by uploading a list from your computer or manually creating a list. To add a new list follow these steps:
1.
Click Add List on the list category menu beneath the function tabs at the top of the page.The Upload List or Manually type List block appears at the bottom of the page.
a.
To Upload a List:
1.
Click Upload List at the top of the box (Figure : Click on the Upload List to Upload a List)
2.
Select the list type from the drop-down menu beside Choose the list type.
3.
Click the browse button beside the Upload file text block. Navigate to the file on your computer that you would like to upload and select it.
4.
Name the list with a unique identifier name, and then click the Add List but­ton. The new list appears in red in the appropriate category.
b.
To create and add a list manually:
1.
Click Manually Type List at the top of the box (Figure : Click on the Manu­ally Type List to Add a List Manually)
2.
Select the list type from the drop-down menu beside Choose the list type.
3.
Enter items into the text block by typing them one to a line.
4.
Name the list with a unique identifier name, and then click the Add List but­ton. The new list will appears in red in the appropriate category.
5.
To open and view the newly created list or save it to your computer, click on the list name in the right-hand column. Click Open or Save.
