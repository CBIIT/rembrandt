Class Comparison Report
The Class Comparison report shows the results from the class comparison performed using the parameters (statistical methods and constraints) set by the user. The report displays group average, where the numerator is the mean of log(base 2) expression signals from the samples in the first group. The denominator is the mean of log(base 2) expression signals from the samples in the second group.
Fold change for the reporter between the selected groups is also displayed along with p-value. If multiple-comparison adjustment is chosen, then adjusted p-value is displayed. Gene symbol annotations are displayed for each reporter. To obtain extensive annotations, click the Excel icon on the upper right-hand corner of the report.
