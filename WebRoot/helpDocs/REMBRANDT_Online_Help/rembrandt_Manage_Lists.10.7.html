Viewing the Data Items in a List
To view the individual data items on a list, follow these steps:
1.
At the top of the Manage List page, click on the type of lists you would like to view (PatientDID Lists, Gene Lists, Reporter Lists).
2.
Find a list to be viewed, and click the details icon to display all of the items in the list.
Note:
The side bar displays each list type and the associated lists. You can mouse-over a list and display the data items.
To export the list, see Exporting a List.
