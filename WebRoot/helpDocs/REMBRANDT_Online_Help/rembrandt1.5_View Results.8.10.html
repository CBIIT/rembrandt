Showing Additional Information
When results are listed in a report, row or column items may appear as links. These links may be for genes, reporters, cytobands, or sample names. Click the link to display additional information about the item.
For example, to display more information about a gene, click the gene symbol link.
The Cancer Genome Anatomy Project (CGAP) browser opens.
