Show Log2 Gene Expression Plot Details
The show Log2 Gene Expression Plot displays average expression intensities for the gene of interest based on Affymetrix GeneChip arrays (U133 Plus 2.0 arrays). Multiple probesets (for some genes) are designed to measure the expression of the gene of interest. For more information on the probe set design strategy for human genes, go to http://www.affymetrix.com.
Group average (samples average based on tumor subtypes in six categories, Glioblastoma Multiforme, Oligodendroglioma, Astrocytoma, Mixed, Unclassified, and Unknown tumors) is calculated for each probeset and is plotted on the Y-axis for each tumor type. To indicate the probability of obtaining the differences in expression values between tumor (or a subtype of tumor) and non-tumor samples, a p-value is displayed with mouse-overs.
Point to a bar on the graph to see Additional Information. The following table describes Additional Information details.

The geometric mean value calculated for each comparison group using t-test.
p-value
The probability for obtaining the differences in expression values between tumor (or a subtype of tumor) and non-tumor samples.
Std. Dev.
The standard deviation value of a comparison group, such as GBM or Astrocytoma, for a particular probeset or gene. Standard deviation is a statistical measure of spread or variability.
