Managing Lists Overview
The Rembrandt List Management function (Figure : Managing Lists Page) centralizes all activities pertaining to the creation and management of user-defined as well as study-defined lists. These lists can be used to further refine queries or to facilitate analysis. Lists can be saved from a query report page, uploaded from another computer, or manually created.
Rembrandt lets you manage three types of lists:
•
PatientDID Lists
•
Gene Lists
•
Reporter Lists
The right-hand column of the Manage Lists page gives the names of the lists that you can manage. (Figure :Lists That You Can Manage) You can also click on the category list menu beneath the tabbed menu (Figure: Click on Category Name to View Lists for that Category) to view the lists for each category.
For each type of list you can perform the following functions:
•
View list details
•
Create custom lists by deleting Items or lists or adding lists
•
Create custom lists from existing lists, by joining lists (Join), creating lists of similar IDs (Intersecting) or lists of non-intersecting IDs (Different).
