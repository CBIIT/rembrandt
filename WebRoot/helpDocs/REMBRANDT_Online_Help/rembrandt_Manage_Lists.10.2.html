Managing Lists Overview
The REMBRANDT Manage Lists function centralizes all activities pertaining to the creation and management of user-defined, as well as study-defined, PatientDID lists, Gene lists, and Reporter lists. With these lists, you can further refine queries or facilitate analysis. When working with lists on the Manage Lists page, you can minimize the number of lists displayed by clicking the PatientDID Lists, Gene Lists, and Reporters Lists heading.
Note:
To add a saved gene or IMAGE clone list to an advanced gene expression search. You can also add a saved Gene List or probeset list to an advanced copy number search.
