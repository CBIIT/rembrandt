Gene Expression Simple Search
To create a gene expression plot, follow these steps:
1.
From the Simple Search page, select Gene Keyword.
If you do not enter a valid gene symbol, the following message appears: The gene you entered is either invalid, or not in the database. Please select another. Close the message window, and enter another gene symÂ­bol.
2.
Enter a gene keyword, for example, enter a HUGO gene symbol such as EGFR or WT1, to plot a gene expression profile based on the expression of your gene of interest.
3.
Click the Go button.
For information about the Gene Expression Plot, see Understanding a Gene Expression Plot.
